About Me

I've spent my entire life being devoted to a love of technology and exploring possibilites for creating fresh content with cutting edge tools and paradigms. Computers have been modern day magic to me since as far back as I can remember and constantly do all I can to continue learning how to use and develop for them. Whether it's doing web development in Javascript, writing my own scripts for Linux, building electronic projects, crafting sound design and audio recording, or fine tuning my headshots in the latest online multiplayer games. The digital world has always been my true home and I want to do what I can to keep it growing and evolving.

I adamantly believe that the World Wide Web is and will continue to be the boldest creation for bringing people together across the planet. Which is why I strongly believe in an ever changing digital age that everyone deserves the right to their privacy and digital freedom. I've also helped launch digital and privacy rights movements such as Keep Our Net Free which has worked in cooperation with major internet privacy groups like Private Internet Access and currently has nearly 30k subscribers on Reddit.
